Project Name: Ummie's Essence 
Primary Market: Kenya
Business Model: Single Vendor
Version: 1.0

1. Executive Summary

Build a premium, animation-driven, high-performance e-commerce website for selling perfumes and cosmetics in Kenya. The platform must support M-Pesa payments, inventory tracking, analytics, promotional tools, and heavy interactive animations while maintaining performance and scalability.

2. Goals & Objectives
Primary Goals

Launch premium online store

Accept M-Pesa as primary payment method

Deliver luxury brand perception

Enable inventory and operational control

Achieve <2.5s page load time

Secondary Goals

Prepare system to scale to 100k users/month

Enable future international shipping

Allow future mobile app integration

3. Target Users
1️⃣ Retail Customers (Primary)
